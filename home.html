The Beauty of Wadi Soon

Wadi Soon is one of the most beautiful valleys in Pakistan, located in the Khushab District. It is famous for its breathtaking scenery, green hills, and peaceful environment. The valley offers a perfect escape from busy city life, where visitors can enjoy fresh air, natural landscapes, and calm surroundings. Many tourists visit Wadi Soon to experience its beauty, relax in nature, and spend quality time with family and friends.

Weather of Wadi Soon

Wadi Soon is known for its pleasant and refreshing weather throughout the year, making it a popular destination for visitors. During summer, the temperature remains moderate compared to many other parts of Pakistan, while winter brings a cool and peaceful atmosphere. Rainfall enhances the beauty of the valley by making hills greener, filling lakes, and refreshing the overall landscape.

Daman-e-Mahar, Soon Valley

Daman-e-Mahar is a peaceful and scenic location in the Soon Valley, District Khushab, Punjab, Pakistan. It lies near the Sakesar mountain range, surrounded by elevated hills. The area offers fresh air, clean water, and a calm environment away from busy city life, making it a refreshing destination for visitors.

Most people in Soon Valley are Zamindars.

They are farmers and landowners.

They live a simple and hardworking life.
