🌿 Kanhatti Garden – Wadi Soon 🌿

Kanhatti Garden is one of the most attractive and peaceful tourist spots in Wadi Soon Valley, located in the Khushab District of Pakistan. Surrounded by lush green hills, natural landscapes, and fresh air, this beautiful garden offers a perfect escape from busy and noisy city life. The calm and refreshing environment makes it an ideal destination for families, tourists, and nature lovers who want to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature.

The garden is well known for its greenery, open spaces, and scenic views. Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks, take photographs, and spend quality time with their loved ones. It is also a great place for picnics and small gatherings. During the spring season, the beauty of Kanhatti Garden increases even more as flowers bloom and the entire area looks colorful and vibrant.

In winter, the cool weather and foggy atmosphere create a unique and relaxing experience for visitors. Kanhatti Garden is not just a place to visit, but a complete natural experience that gives peace of mind and freshness to the soul. It truly represents the hidden beauty of Wadi Soon and remains one of the must-visit locations for anyone exploring this region.

📍 Wadi Soon Sakesar

Soon Valley is one of the most beautiful areas in Punjab, Pakistan. Sakesar is the highest peak, offering breathtaking views, lush greenery, and a peaceful environment for visitors and nature lovers.

📍 View Full Location

🏔️ Sakesar Height

Sakesar, the highest peak of Soon Valley, stands approximately 1,522 meters (4,993 feet) above sea level. This impressive height makes it one of the most prominent points in the Salt Range, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. The cool climate and peaceful environment attract tourists, nature lovers, and photographers throughout the year.

🏔️ Maiwali Dheri

Maiwali Dheri is a beautiful hilltop viewpoint in Soon Valley, located at approximately 900–1,100 meters above sea level. From this height, cool fresh winds blow from all directions, creating a peaceful and refreshing atmosphere. From this point, almost the entire Wadi Soon can be seen, including its green hills and wide plains. The panoramic view and natural beauty make it a perfect place for visitors and nature lovers.

🌊 Uchali Lake – Wadi Soon 🌊

Uchali Lake is located in the Soon Valley (Wadi Soon) of Khushab District in Punjab, Pakistan. It lies about 30 kilometers from Naushera and is surrounded by green hills and natural landscapes. The lake is famous for its saltwater, peaceful environment, and scenic beauty. It is easily accessible by road, and the journey itself offers beautiful views, making it a popular destination for tourists and nature lovers.
